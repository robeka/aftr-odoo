Is this app compatible with Odoo Enterprise as well as Community?

Yes, the app works perfectly well with Odoo Enterprise (On-premise) as well as Community. Odoo Online (Cloud) does not allow installation of third-party apps and hence this app cannot be installed on Odoo Online.

I need an extra feature in this app. How can I request it?

You can contact us to request a customization in the app

Do I get free upgrades and support if I purchase the app?

If you purchase our app for Odoo Version 12 and prior, we will provide you access to all the versions up to 12 depending on their availability. Starting version 13, every version of the app will be sold separately.

We offer free support for a duration of 90 days from the date of purchase. Kindly read our support policy to know the details.

What does 'Weight of Shipping' mean?

If you prefer to make packages in Odoo and put one or more products in the packages, the packaging weight will be calculated separately and then added to the actual weight of the Product. The total weight (product + package) is termed as 'Weight of Shipping'.

Can I change the delivery address after Delivery Order is processed?

No, it is not possible to change the recipient address once your Delivery Order is processed in Odoo.

If I pack the whole order into multiple packages, will I get the shipping label for each individual package?

Yes, you will get the shipping label for each individual package. Shipping Label will also be generated for products which are a part of order but not included in the packages.

How a customer can track the order from Odoo Website?

The customer can track the latest shipment status from their account on the Website under the 'My Account / Order' section. Moreover, when the Delivery Order is validated, the customer will get an Email with the tracking link if ‘Auto Send Shipment Mail’ is checked while configuring the shipping instance.

Can I cancel the shipment after Delivery Order is processed & label is generated?

Yes, it is possible to cancel the shipment after your Delivery Order is processed. Just click on 'Cancel' button beside the tracking number in 'Additional Info' tab of Sale Order. However, if the shipping provider has already shipped your packages to the customer, then it will not be practically possible to cancel the shipment.

What is FedEx OneRate service?

FedEx One Rate is flat-rate shipping that doesn’t require you to weigh or measure shipments under 50 lbs. You can add to the box without adding to the price. More information about FedEx OneRate service is available here.

Does this app allow the selection of Drop Off Type services of FedEx?

Yes, this app allows the selection of various Drop Off Type services offered by FedEx such as Regular Pickup, Regular Courier, Drop Box, Business Service Center and Station.

Do I need to create separate instances for Domestic & International services of FedEx?

No, Domestic & International services will be available in the same instance. There is no need to create a separate instance for each of them.

My UoM for Weight in Odoo is different from FedEx's standard UoM. Will it affect the shipping prices?

No, it will not affect the shipping prices. Just set the same Weight of UoM in both parameters while configuring the Delivery Methods. Our app will automatically convert the UoM while sharing the weight of package with UPS. For eg., KG(S) to LB(P).

Can I create product-specific shipping fees?

No, you cannot create product-specific shipping fees.

How can the customers see & choose the FedEx Shipping Services on my Odoo Website?

At the time of checkout, the customer will see a list of FedEx services (Delivery Methods) which are marked as ‘Published on Website’ in Odoo. They will also be able to see the rates associated with each delivery method and choose whichever option they find appropriate.

I have multiple FedEx accounts. Can I connect them all with a single Odoo Instance using this connector?

This connector will help you manage multiple FedEx accounts in a single Odoo Instance. You can create individual Shipping Instances for each of your FedEx accounts and manage various operations from a single Odoo environment.

Can I attach my company logo in shipping label generated by FedEx?

By default, you cannot attach company logo in the shipping label generated by FedEx. However, if you need this feature, please contact us to make a customization request.
